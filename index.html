Tipps & Strategien

Ideas to keep you motivated and immersed.

The 80/20 Rule

Don't stress about perfect grammar for every single sentence. 20% of the vocabulary allows you to understand 80% of daily conversation. Focus on communication first, accuracy second.

Change Your Phone Language

It sounds annoying, but changing your phone's interface to German forces you to learn practical, technical vocabulary (Settings, Network, Battery, etc.) passively every day.

Talk to Yourself

Narrate your day. "Ich mache jetzt Kaffee." "Der Bus ist sp√§t." It feels silly, but it bridges the gap between thinking and speaking.

German Media Consumption

Watch "Tagesschau" in 100 seconds. Listen to "Easy German" podcasts. Immersion is key to understanding the rhythm of the language.

The "No English" Hour

Designate one hour where you are forbidden to speak, write, or think in English. If you don't know the word, circumscribe it (describe it using other words).
